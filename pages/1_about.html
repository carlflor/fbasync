Our Story

Our home started in 2015 as an offshoot session of the growing the Feast Bay Area, a Catholic gathering of the Light of Jesus Family, under the leadership of Bro. Bo Sanchez.

First known as the Feast Bay Area SYNC (which had stood for Singles and Youth in Christ), its first builder was Bro. Migs Ramirez. This feast was intended to be the home Feast of the singles and youth of the Feast Bay Area. When God called Bro. Migs to the priestly vocation, another leader was appointed for the FBA SYNC.

In July 2016, Bro Didoy became the builder of the Sync. With the inspiration of the Holy Spirit, the FBA Sync was redirected to a more encompassing mission: to make disciples by connecting with God and with one another. It has become the home Feast not only of the singles and youth of the Feast Bay Area, but more so of everyone who needs Christ.

By December 2017, with the growth of the Sync family, our home transitioned to the Mall of Asia. Our home has now become the Feast Mall of Asia, where Jesus takes the center stage and where you are loved.

Our Culture

Culture of Worship

We believe that the Celebration of the Eucharist is the highest form of worship. We believe that worship is a beautiful gift from God that helps us to have a deeper and personal relationship with Him. We believe that worship is lived out as a daily lifestyle.

Culture of Friendship

We welcome everybody. If you are searching, yearning, hurting, or just about anything, you have a family here. We look out for one another. We celebrate and cry together. You can make us your home.

Culture of Stewardship

We believe that whatever God has given to us, we take care of it. God deserves nothing but excellence. We strive to bring out the best in oneself and in one another. We believe in showing "malasakit" to every person and to every thing that we do.

Culture of Leadership

We believe in empowering leadership. Leaders create more leaders. We operate on mutual trust, with love and humility as our primary values in the Light of Jesus Family.
